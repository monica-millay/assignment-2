Correct order to eat a lobster

in my humble opinion as someone from Maine

Click the buttons below to reveal the correct order:

FIRST
the tail
Easy to open and delicious. An obvious first choice.
SECOND
the big (a.k.a. "crusher") claw
Harder to open and less meat than the tail, but arguably more delicious.
THIRD
the smaller (a.k.a "pincher") claw
Easier to open than the crusher, but way less meat. Still delicious.
FOURTH
the legs
Only at this point are you desparate enough to try to suck the meat out of the legs. Hardly ever works anyway.

*I don't normally eat the remaining parts, but some very hard-core Mainers do.
